How to play:
  • Deplete Err's health bar to defeat it!
  • Turn alternates between attacking and defending.
  • Choose 5 cards to click or drag to the play area in the center.
  • The combination of cards you choose determine the strength of your move!
  • 5 cards of the same suit form special moves!
Hand (value): Cards needed
  1. Royal Force (Fixed 200): Same suit, A 10 J Q K
  2. Supreme Force (+50): Same suit, 5 in a sequence
  3. Four of a kind (+40): 4 same numbers
  4. Full House (+30): 3 of a number and a pair of another number
  5. Forces (+25): Same suit, any cards
  6. Straight (+20): 5 cards in a sequence
  7. Three of a kind (+15): 3 same numbers
  8. Two Pairs(+10): Pair of same numbers and another pair of same numbers
  9. Pair (+5): 2 same numbers
  10. High Card (no bonus)
